Purchases Policy
Purchases are valid for 30 days from the date of purchase and receipt of payment.
If your order is not delivered within two weeks, please write an email to KasiThreads, and we will follow up on the order.
If, after two weeks of contacting KasiThreads, you have not received your order, you may request a refund by following the proper procedures.
Return Policy
From the date you receive your order, you have two working days to lodge a complaint about the product.
Returns will be valid only for the following reasons: damaged goods, incorrect size, or incorrect item.
To initiate a return, please write to KasiThreads, providing pictures of the product and your customer number.
KasiThreads will respond within two working days.
Transportation costs for the return will be covered by KasiThreads, and you will receive a 10% discount on your next use of our services.
Transportation Policy
The website offers two transportation methods: Aramex store to door and Paxi.
Aramex store to door delivery ensures your product will arrive within three working days at a cost of R100.
Paxi offers two options: delivery at R100 within 3-5 business days or at R60 within 7-9 business days.
